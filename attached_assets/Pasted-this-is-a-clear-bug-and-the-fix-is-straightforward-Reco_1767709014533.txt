Edge-cases & validation

Size limit: 1–2 MB per signature is more than enough; enforce on client and server.
MIME types: accept PNG/JPEG/SVG only.
Idempotency: once signature_url exists, block or require replace confirmation.
Audit trail: log IP, timestamp (you already do), and also record signature_uploaded_at and uploader ID.
Retention/compliance: if signatures are legally sensitive, set retention policy and restrict access. Consider encryption at rest (Supabase/S3 can handle server-side encryption).
Historical data: signatures already lost cannot be recovered. If proof is required, have volunteers re-sign or add an “I agree” checkbox plus a re-sign campaign.
