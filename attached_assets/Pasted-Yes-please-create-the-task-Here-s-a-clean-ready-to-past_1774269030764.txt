Task #34 - Add Director Role to Role Assignment UI
What & Why
The "director" role exists in the database schema and is correctly wired for full schedule-builder access, but it cannot be assigned through the UI. Admins must currently edit the database directly to grant it. This task adds "director" as a selectable option in both the role management screen and the invitation dropdown so school admins can assign it safely without touching the database.
