Patriots Class (9th–12th graders, ages 14–18)
Class Description
Patriots Class is designed for 9th–12th graders (ages 14–18), deepening reading, writing, and critical thinking while building self-governance, character, entrepreneurship, financial literacy and appreciation for America's founding principles in a warm, mentor-led setting.
Classes meet Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, 9 AM–12 PM, with optional extended learning until 2 PM. Sessions are 10 weeks long and offered three times per year, creating a consistent routine that boosts focus and excitement.
Math and literacy use station-based learning to meet each student's level. Paired reading strengthens decoding, comprehension, and confidence through peer support.
Math follows Dimensions Math (Singapore-inspired). Each student gets a home workbook for practice and a traveling textbook for lessons, so parents can easily follow along and help with homework.
ASA fosters independent thinkers in a positive, social environment combining structured curriculum, liberal arts, hands-on projects and discussions emphasizing responsibility, critical thinking, and free-market ideas.

Yankee Doodles Class (PreK–Kindergarten, ages 4–5)
Class Description
Yankee Doodles Class is thoughtfully designed for PreK to Kindergarten (ages 4–5), building strong foundational skills in literacy, counting, and early responsibility through a gentle classical education approach.
A warm, meaningful connection with a dedicated, trained instructor provides the mentorship young children thrive on—especially valuable for homeschooling families.
Classes meet Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, 9 AM–12 PM (optional extension to 2 PM). Sessions are 10 weeks long and offered three times per year, creating a predictable rhythm that helps little ones feel secure and excited to learn.
Hands-on, movement-rich activities spark curiosity and joy while introducing letters, sounds, phonemic awareness, number sense, shapes, colors, and simple routines of responsibility and kindness. Play-based learning fosters independence, social skills in a nurturing, community focused environment.
Yankee Doodles empowers homeschooling parents with a balanced, joyful program that nurtures confident, eager learners and virtuous little citizens—blending academics, creativity, character, and fun in a mentor-guided space.

Tycoons Class (1st–2nd graders, ages 6–8)
Class Description
Tycoons Class is tailored for 1st–2nd graders (ages 6–8), building strong skills in reading, writing, and math while nurturing character, creativity, and independence in a structured, engaging setting.
Classes meet Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, 9 AM–12 PM, with optional extended learning until 2 PM. Sessions are 10 weeks long and offered three times per year, creating a consistent routine that boosts focus and excitement.
Math and literacy use station-based learning to meet each child's level. Students rotate through targeted activities: some use controlled-text (decodable) readers to build confidence, others advance with uncontrolled-text books for fluency. Paired reading strengthens decoding, comprehension, and confidence through peer support.
Math follows Dimensions Math (Singapore-inspired), covering number sense, addition/subtraction within 100, place value, shapes, and strategies like number bonds and bar models. Each child gets a home workbook for practice and a traveling textbook for lessons, so parents can easily follow along and help with homework.
Tycoons Class empowers young learners to become confident thinkers, responsible citizens, and lifelong explorers—blending rigorous academics with character-building and hands-on fun!

• Dimensions Math Textbook and Workbook (assessed in class)
• Orthography Notebook (annually)
• Uncontrolled Books (based on Lexile score)
• Take Home Folder
• 9x12 perforated sketchbook
Macaronis Class (ages 6 months–4 years)
Class Description
Our Macaronis Class is a joyful program that welcomes our youngest learners (from ages 6 months up to 4 years) into a warm, nurturing academic setting designed to spark curiosity, encourage sensory exploration, and build foundational social skills. Beyond engaging lessons on numbers, letters, colors, and syllables, your child will be amused with a structured and vibrant morning filled with play, music, movement, obstacle courses, classic stories, science demos and enriching arts and crafts—for three whole hours, three days a week.
A consistent, repetitive schedule fosters routine and excitement. The class typically features:
• Circle time to connect with friends and kick off the adventure
• Music and movement with lively songs and dances to build coordination and rhythm
• Rhymes and songs to boost language development and phonological awareness
• Playful introductions to letters, numbers, shapes, and colors through interactive games
• Math and literacy are practiced during educational stations and fun activities with differentiated lessons for PreK-ready learners.
• Science lab inspires wonder and imagination with weekly hands on experiments and demonstrations.
Macaroni's is the ideal blend of active play, socialization, exploration, and foundational lesson that help prepare your child with the desire to learn!
